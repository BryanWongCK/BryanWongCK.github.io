Tips:
    Game length is expect to be short, around 5mins.
        3min for difficulty to be moderate
        5min for difficulty to be hard
    Collect Xp dropped from enemies to get stronger
    Enemies scale over time so dont fall behind
    Swapping rotation quickly will steady your aim
    Goal is to survive as long as possible
